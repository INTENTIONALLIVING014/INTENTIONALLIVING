OUR INTENTIONAL CAMPUS

A Living Laboratory for Purposeful Design

Our campus in MANSA, Zambia is a model of intentional living, integrating sustainable architecture with natural environments. Located at Mwatakazembe Road, Plot number 367, every building and space is designed with purpose and mindfulness.
