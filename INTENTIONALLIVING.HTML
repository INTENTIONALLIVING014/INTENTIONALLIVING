Welcome to INTENTIONAL LIVING

Design your life with purpose at Zambia's premier institution for intentional design. Our unique approach integrates physical spaces, technology, and earth sciences to create environments that nurture growth and well-being.

Located in Mansa, Zambia, our campus serves as a living laboratory where students learn to design environments that enhance quality of life through purposeful design.

Our Philosophy

At INTENTIONAL LIVING University, we believe that every space should be designed with intention. Our programs teach students to create environments that:

  • Enhance well-being and productivity
  • Integrate sustainable technologies
  • Harmonize with natural environments
  • Inspire creativity and purpose
